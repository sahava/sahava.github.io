Hydrabit Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 6, 2026

Hydrabit is designed with privacy in mind. Your health data stays on your device.

Information We Collect

Hydrabit collects and stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Hydration logs (amount and time of water intake)
  • Micro-break completions (standing, stretching, eye breaks)
  • Your preferences and settings

How Your Data Is Stored

All data is stored locally on your iPhone and Apple Watch using Apple's secure on-device storage. Your data is:

  • Never uploaded to external servers
  • Never shared with third parties
  • Never used for advertising or analytics
  • Synced only between your own devices via Apple's secure Watch Connectivity framework

Notifications

Hydrabit uses local notifications to remind you to drink water and take breaks. These notifications are generated and scheduled entirely on your device. No notification data is transmitted externally.

Third-Party Services

Hydrabit does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. We do not collect crash reports or usage data.

Data Deletion

To delete all your Hydrabit data, simply uninstall the app from your iPhone and Apple Watch. Since all data is stored locally, removing the app permanently deletes all associated data.

Children's Privacy

Hydrabit does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app is designed for general audiences and contains no age-restricted content.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
