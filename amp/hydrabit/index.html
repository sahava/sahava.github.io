üí¨ Support & FAQ

How do I set up Apple Watch sync?

Simply install Hydrabit on your iPhone, then open the Watch app and install Hydrabit on your Apple Watch. The apps sync automatically via Bluetooth‚Äîno account or setup required.

Can I change the reminder interval?

Yes! Go to the Habits tab and adjust your reminder interval to 60, 90, or 120 minutes. You can also set your active hours so you're not disturbed outside your preferred times.

What happens if I miss a reminder?

Hydrabit uses a fixed schedule based on your active hours. If you miss a reminder, the next one will still arrive on schedule. You can also snooze for 30 minutes if you need more time.

Is my data private?

Absolutely. All your data stays on your device. We don't collect analytics, don't use tracking, and don't upload your information anywhere. Read our Privacy Policy for full details.

How do I delete my data?

Go to Settings ‚Üí Reset all data to clear your history. Alternatively, uninstalling the app removes all data since everything is stored locally on your device.

Can I export my hydration data?

Yes! Go to Settings ‚Üí Export all app data to download a complete JSON export of your hydration logs and break completions.
